Project Details

Research, Analysis and Documentation.

Smart India Hackathon(SIH) is an initiative taken by the government of India to help students across the country to take part in hackathons and develop an idea. We got an opportunity to chose an idea and we selected "Quick analysis of Pulses, cereals and oilseeds using Artificial Intelligence" and started working on it. We have designed a setup that includes image processing techniques, neural network model and hardware architecture to load and unload the food grains on the setup.

Smart India Hackathon 2020 Winners!
